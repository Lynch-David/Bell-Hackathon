The Animal Guarantee

Animal Stores

Animal stores are very common so people can provide for their pets without travelling far.

Animal Security

It's always a good idea to get some sort of easily trackable device for your pet in case the door is left open or if they are an outside pet.

Animal Categorization

Animals all have their own "subcategories" which is just the different breeds of whichever animal you refer to.

Animal Communication

Animals all communicate in different ways. For example cats only meow as a communication method to humans.

Animal Networks

Animals mostly have their own networks. It is essentially their family/group not every species or animal has a group or family they follow.

Animal Storage

Animals that are to be pets are "stored" in shelters. They are kept in cages depending on their size and are fed and played with by the workers.
